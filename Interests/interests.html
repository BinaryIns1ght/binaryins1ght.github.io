Ways to relax and focus

  • take a short break from your project
  • listen to some videos, or listen to some appropriate music to give yourself energy.
  • look at your project in many perspectives, see what makes sense, see what needs to be changed, added, or omitted
  • organize your work to help with understanding the project better
  • while relaxing, also think about what makes sense to work on in the project.
  • if overwhelmed or on a burnout, best to rest well and make sure to get your mind off of the project.

Design Advice

  • Use grid for organization
  • focus on what is the most important detail on the design
  • have a frame of reference when designing something.
  • in typography, focus on typeface style, tone, and glyph for the appropriate tone.
  • in graphic designs, play around with figure and ground. utilize the visual aspects of the design.
  • focus on the size and "loudness" of the visual aspects of design. from size and color, to shape and tone, it depends on which part of the design is supposed to take focus on.
